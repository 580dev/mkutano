About Event

The Biggest Digital Conference.

It is a gathering of various IT tech profiles, from all over the world, including speakers, who share their experience on the recent aspects of research in their respective fields (Devops, Cloud, Language, Framework, VR, I.A., Blockchain) as well as the approach allowing resilience when using these technologies.

For this first edition, the event will take place entirely online via our LinkedIn and Youtube platforms. Stay connected to be notified in real time
